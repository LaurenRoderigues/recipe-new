Raspberry Champagne Rum Punch

Need a party punch that will get all your friends feeling good? Try this easy to make asethically pleasing, instagram worthy Party Punch!

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 ml) bottle of blush or pink champagne (chilled)
  • 1 liter bottle of ginger ale (chilled)
  • 1 cup light rum
  • 1 (10-ounce) bag frozen raspberries, slightly thawed
  • Optional mint sprigs for garnish

Steps

  1. Step one: Grab a large bowl and fill it with ice.
  2. Step two: Then grab your friends and an Iphone cause it's time to pop the bottle of champagne.
  3. Step Three: Pour both the bottle of rum and champnge at the same time into the bowl of ice. Trust me you are gonna want to snapchat this.
  4. Step Four: Pour the bag of frozen raspberries on top.
  5. Step Five: Sprinkel the mint leaves over the whole bowl. It is very aesthetically pleasing. Be sure to mix it all up with a spoon
  6. Enjoy the night by sippin' your homemade cocktail. Congradulations you are a professional bartender now
